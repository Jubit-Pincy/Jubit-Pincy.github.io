Upcycle the recycle

An e-waste management system

When my teammates and I first started out in search for a topic for our project we came across the e-waste situation in our country and realized that there is a massive amount of e-waste but not a proper way to recycle/reuse it in a way that does not harm the environment.

And so we decided that there can be a system where not all used electronics have to go to a landfill waiting to be picked up and treated right. Our project offers a platform where users can buy or sell even the smallest parts of electronics at a reasonable price. They may also put up the products made of e-waste (like pen holders from keyboard keys) up for sale.

The users will be able to upload their products once they create an account. The products uploaded by the user will only be visible on the site once it has been approved by the admins. This is so that only content relevant to the site are made visible to the users.

The dashboard comes in both light and dark modes. And the entire design is mobile responsive.

A contact form is provided in case the user wants to contact the admins regarding the website or any of the products.

In the project I had mainly worked on the front end development as well as some work in the backend. I worked on the designs and tweaked it until everyone was satisfied with the final product. We discussed what each page would do and I made the front end accordingly.
